Problem 1

A cake is divided and served different ways.

See how the same amount can be shown as different fractions.

↓
1/2
2/4
Division lines appear, splitting each slice in half. The shaded amount stays the same — just cut into more pieces!

Now you try! Click the 2 shaded pieces to show 2/4

Watch the pattern continue...

Same amount shaded every time. Only the number of slices changes. This is what equivalent fractions are! 1/2 = 2/4 = 4/8 = 8/16
